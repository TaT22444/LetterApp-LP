Chocoleta is a message app where you send digital letters — not texts. You write on customizable stationery, attach photos, and pick a font and color. To close the letter, drag an emoji stamp onto the envelope. To send, pull the stamp back like a slingshot and release. Your friend receives a 3D envelope on their screen that they can tilt with their finger. Tap to open — the flap lifts, the paper slides out, and the message appears page by page.
